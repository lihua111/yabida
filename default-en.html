Your Global E-Commerce Logistics Partner

Yabida is revolutionizing global e-commerce logistics through seamless solutions for cross-border e-sellers.

 

We are changing the game in terms of cost efficiency, timeliness, our robust transportation network and our teams of industry experts serving markets around the world. Our commitment to seamless, end-to-end customer experiences is unmatched.

CORE VALUES

Reliability
Timeliness
Cost-efficiency
Digitalization
Sustainability

  • Reliability

    We focus on delivering superior customer experiences by addressing specific needs. We have dedicated implementation teams that support customers at every stage of their logistics journey. We also partner with the best local carriers to guarantee smooth last-mile delivery. We show up for customers with 10 years of sector experience under our belt.

  • Timeliness

    We strive for operational excellence in every aspect of our service, including G2G (Global to Global) products, return handling, sorting and road transport. Yabida Europe provides services in over 30 European countries, with established branches in 21 of them. We operate our own logistics hubs, customs clearance hubs, transport routes and sorting centers to seamlessly connect Europe with the rest of the world.

  • Cost-efficiency

    We add value to your global business by providing a variety of tailored logistics solutions, according to your needs and budget. We offer our services at competitive prices that make our superior solutions stand out against other cross-border providers in Europe. With no surprise fees or fuel surcharges at any stage of the process.

  • Digitalization

    Our in-house developers continually refine our cutting-edge IT systems. We implement advanced, real-time GPS tracking systems and embrace new technologies, such as RFID and Blockchain. And our Smart Sorting Hub sorts from ULD to pallet to parcel, and every level in between. We are leading the charge in the digital transformation of the global transportation network.

  • Sustainability

    We are committed to green logistics. Our systems are future-proof, and social responsibility is a key factor in the decision-making that drives our solutions. Our warehouses meet the standard of sustainability level of ‘Excellent’.
